Let's play

What is PolyCity

This is a place where you can build your dream city, where you can let your imagination fly and you can also trial your strategy skills. Build a happy, well organized city with balancing the statistics such as purchase request, working places and entertainment, according to the population.

How To Play

To choose the space of a building, use your arrow keys! The up and down keys are for moving, the left and right arrows are for rotating the the picker object.
If you picked the place where you want to add your building, choose its type at the side bar, and click on it!
Now, you have a building and roads around it.
You can add buildings this way, anywhere on the board. If you want to grow the play area, just place a building at the edge of the board, it will collapse the land size a bit.
You can also add cars, but just if the picker object is on a road. You don't want to crash the buildings with speeding vehicles, don't you?
While you are building the city of your dreams, take care of the people's wishes too. Make sure the city has the right amount of working places, shopping centers and the ability to have fun and live a wonderful life under your leadership! :)
